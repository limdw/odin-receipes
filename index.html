Laksa Link

Description

Laksa is my favourite dish lah!

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • Noodles
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chilli Paste
  • Curry Paste
    •

    Steps

    1. Remove head and shells from prawns, devein and set prawn meats aside in another bowl.
    2. For the prawn stock, add cooking oil to a large saucepan on medium-high heat. Add prawn heads and shells then stir for 5minutes, crushing the heads to release the flavour.
    3. add water and chicken stock, then simmer for 45minutes allowing the stock to reduce.
    4. Remove prawn shells then strain the broth into a bowl through a sieve.
    5. Add dried chilli, red chilli, dried shrimp, shrimp paste(belecan), garlic etc into a food processor and pulse for 5minutes till a thick paste forms.
    6. Add cooking oil into a large saucepan and then add laksa paste and cook for 5minutes till fragrant. Add palm sugar, fish sauce, coconut milk and prawn stock stirring to combine. Simmer the mixture for 15minutes then add prawns and tofu puffs, cooking for 3minutes to finish.
